Hello Welcome! ( Bem Vindos-Portuguese, Bienvenidos-Spanish)

A few years ago, I embarked on the wonderful adventure of learning Software Engineering in English. After having learned computer programming in Portuguese years ago (yes, quite a difference!), I decided to dive into this new field. And let me tell you, it was quite a ride! In the end, it was worth it—I completed the program in April 2024.

Here’s what I’ve learned:

1. It’s Possible:

Yes, it’s entirely possible to learn Software Engineering, Computer Programming, and much more from the comfort of your home, even if you’ve never studied these subjects before. Answering the question I previously raised and published on this site..

2. Perseverance Pays Off:

It won’t be easy, but don’t give up. The journey is worth it. Remember, learning is a continuous journey, and every step counts! Don't rush through the program just to finish it. Make sure to take your time to learn it well, at a pace that fits you. You can find information on reputable programs to enroll in on platforms like: Coursera, edX, Udacity, and Udemy. These online global platforms offer life-changing learning experiences, granting access to courses and degrees from leading universities and companies. Whether you speak Portuguese, Spanish, English, or any other language, there’s likely a program out there for you.

3. Upgrade Your Skills:

In this era of artificial intelligence advancements, it’s crucial to upgrade your computer skills. Keeping up with the speed of technology is essential.

4. Gratitude to Educators:

A heartfelt thank you to all software teachers and instructors. Their dedication and love for educating others are priceless.
