Overcoming Rock Bottom

Have you ever felt like you've lost your strength? Life can lead us to the proverbial “rock bottom.” But even in those darkest moments, there’s hope.

• Adverse Childhood Experiences: Children reporting traumatic events need support. Trauma can resurface later in life.

• Life Transitions: Divorce, job loss, or addictions can destabilize us.

• Believe in Change: Change is possible. There is light at the end of the tunnel.

• Perseverance: Keep looking for the exit of the maze.
